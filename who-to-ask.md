# I Don't Know Who to Ask

When you're not sure who handles something, start here. Most of these point you to the right My RJ tile or the right phone option.

### Q: I don't even know who to ask. Where do I start?

Start with the **My RJ Resource Board**. It has tiles for Academics, Athletics, Student & School Policies, Books/Devices/Apps, School Counseling, Food Service, Transportation, and more. If you still don't know where to go, ask your counselor or message **Mr. Beyer on Teams** and say, "I'm not sure who handles this."

**Where to go:** [My RJ Resource Board](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboard)

### Q: Who do I ask if my schedule looks wrong?

Start with the **Academics** tile in My RJ. Schedule and course questions go to **Mr. Ryan Williamson**, Assistant Principal for Academics, at **303-269-8118** or **rwilliamson@regisjesuit.com**. Course changes aren't automatic, and changes based only on teacher preference aren't considered.

**Where to go:** [Academics tile](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboarddetail/26269)

### Q: What is the tool RJ uses for service hours?

RJ logs Ignatian Immersion & Solidarity hours in the **MobileServe** app — you use it to record volunteer hours, get supervisor verification by email, and check whether hours are pending, approved, or rejected. Sign in with the **Microsoft button** (not a username/password) so it connects to your RJ account. The **Ignatian Immersion & Solidarity** page in My RJ has the full instructions and approved opportunities. (Step-by-step on the Clubs, Sports & Culture page.)

**Where to go:** [Ignatian Immersion & Solidarity](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#communitypage/96661874/bulletinboard)

### Q: Who do I ask if I'm stressed, lost, or not okay?

Start with your counselor or a trusted adult — and call 911 if it's urgent. Find your assigned counselor in **My RJ → School Counseling**, or call **303-269-8000, Option 5**. You don't need to wait until something is a crisis. (See the Wellbeing & Safety page for crisis resources.)

**Where to go:** [Resource Board](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboard) → School Counseling
