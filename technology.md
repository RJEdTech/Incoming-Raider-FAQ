# Technology

Your Dell, your accounts, Wi-Fi, Canvas, and what to do when something breaks. For step-by-step device setup, RaiderQuest is still your main guide — this page is for the questions that pop up around it.

### Q: Is it bad if I've never really used a Windows laptop before?

No. A lot of incoming freshmen are coming from Chromebooks, iPads, or Macs. RaiderQuest covers the basics in **Mission 2: Knowing Your Dell**, and the Windows reference explains the big moves like the Start menu, File Explorer, right-click, and keyboard shortcuts. If you get stuck during RaiderQuest, message **Mr. Beyer on Teams**; for device or login problems, contact the **IT Helpdesk**.

### Q: So OneDrive backs up my stuff… but what does that actually mean?

It means your school files should save to OneDrive instead of being stuck only on your laptop. Every file saved there is backed up to Microsoft OneDrive, so a lost or broken laptop doesn't mean lost work. Save schoolwork in the folders connected to OneDrive, not in random places you might forget.

### Q: What if I lose my Dell?

Tell **RJ IT** right away — Room 166D, **303-269-8080**, **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com**, or the Helpdesk ticket site. ### Q: I forgot my password. Is that the same as forgetting my PIN?

No. Your RJ **password** signs you into Microsoft services and can be reset at the password reset site. Your Windows Hello **PIN** only unlocks that one laptop; if you forgot just the PIN, use **"I forgot my PIN"** on the Windows sign-in screen. If a reset doesn't work, contact the **IT Helpdesk** at **303-269-8080** or **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com**.

**Where to go:** [Reset your password](https://passwordreset.microsoftonline.com/)

### Q: Why do I need Microsoft Authenticator if I picked Windows Hello or a security key?

Authenticator is your backup for account recovery. It helps if your device fails, your security key is lost, or you need to get back into your account another way. If your family needs to change your sign-in method, email **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com**.

### Q: Does RJ get a scan of my face if I use Windows Hello?

No. Regis Jesuit does not collect, access, or store your biometric data. Windows Hello turns your face data into an encrypted template that stays only on your laptop, locked in the device's secure hardware — RJ can't get to it. For setup or sign-in trouble, contact the **IT Helpdesk** at **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com** or **303-269-8080**.

### Q: Can I put my phone or personal laptop on RJ-Wireless?

No. Personal devices like phones, tablets, and personal electronics aren't permitted on RJ-Wireless, and RJ-Guest is for visitors, not students. If your school device is having Wi-Fi trouble, contact the **IT Helpdesk** instead of hopping onto other networks.

### Q: Can I install whatever apps I want on my school laptop?

Not freely. You don't have admin rights on a school device, and trying to get around that can become a discipline issue. Approved apps are installed through the **Company Portal** app; if a class needs something that isn't in Company Portal, ask your teacher, then send an academic-reason request to the **IT Helpdesk** at **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com** or **303-269-8080**.

### Q: How do students print at school?

You can print in the **library**, which has both black-and-white and color printers. Please print double-sided when you can. The library is open **Monday–Friday, 7:30 AM–4:00 PM**.

### Q: Canvas says my assignment is missing, but I swear I submitted it. What should I check?

Open the assignment and look for the submission confirmation or timestamp. If nothing is listed, your submission probably didn't go through, so resubmit if Canvas still allows it. If a tech problem blocked you, email your **teacher** and **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com before the due date**.

### Q: RaiderQuest is locked and I don't know why. Who do I ask?

First check the **Modules** page for anything without a green checkmark — some pages need you to click **Mark as done** before the next part unlocks. If you've done that and it's still stuck, message **Mr. Beyer on Teams**, since RaiderQuest and Canvas onboarding are Educational Technology.

### Q: My Dell setup at home is stuck. Do I just keep waiting?

Some setup screens can take **30–60 minutes**, so a long wait is sometimes normal. If it's stuck on the same screen for more than **an hour**, contact the **IT Helpdesk** at **303-269-8080** or **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com**. If you get an Admin Device Compliance email, forward it to the Helpdesk.

### Q: I'm scared I'll mess up the laptop. What are the big things not to do?

Keep it protected when you carry it, charge it nightly, store it securely, and keep it away from food and drinks. If something gets damaged or stops working, report it right away instead of hiding it. For help, go to **RJ IT Support in Boys Division, Room 166D** or email **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com**.
