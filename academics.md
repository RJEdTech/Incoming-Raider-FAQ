# Academics

Homework, Canvas, grades, GPA, makeup work, and the rules around AI and academic honesty. For class-specific questions, your teacher is always the best first stop.

### Q: How much homework should I expect?

RJ expects you to work both at school and at home. The Handbook recommends doing homework the evening it's assigned — even if that class doesn't meet the next day — so you still have time to ask for help. Use Academic Support early if you start falling behind.

### Q: What's the difference between late, missing, and locked in Canvas?

**Late** means you submitted after the due date. **Missing** means the due date passed and Canvas shows no submission. **Locked** means the assignment is no longer open for submissions unless your teacher reopens it.

**Where to go:** [Canvas Student Guide — Assignments](https://community.canvaslms.com/t5/Student-Guide/How-do-I-view-Assignments-as-a-student/ta-p/420)

### Q: What does "Available Until" mean in Canvas?

"Available Until" is the lock date. After that date, the Submit button may disappear even though the assignment still exists. If that happens, only your teacher can decide whether to reopen it.

### Q: What is Copyleaks?

Copyleaks is the tool RJ uses inside Canvas to scan some submissions for similarity and AI-generated writing. Similarity and AI detection are separate scores. A report is information for the teacher to review — it isn't something this FAQ can judge for you.

**Where to go:** [Copyleaks — Canvas Student overview](https://help.copyleaks.com/s/article/CanvasStudent681cc7aa4d5b2)

### Q: What does a Copyleaks color mean?

The colored bubble usually shows the **similarity score**, not the AI score. Green means a small amount of matching text, yellow moderate, orange significant, and red very high. Your teacher reviews the report along with the context.

**Where to go:** [Copyleaks — color meanings](https://help.copyleaks.com/s/article/WhatdothedifferentcolorsontheCanvasassignmentmean681cd1ef3d2b5)

Don't try to argue with the score or rewrite your story. Gather evidence of your process — version history, drafts, notes, sources — then talk to your teacher. If the issue is technical evidence, message **Mr. Beyer on Teams** or contact **HelpDesk@regisjesuit.com**.

**Where to go:** [Copyleaks — Canvas Student overview](https://help.copyleaks.com/s/article/CanvasStudent681cc7aa4d5b2)

### Q: What if Copyleaks says I used AI but I didn't?

### Q: Is a high Copyleaks score automatically plagiarism?

No. A high similarity score can happen for several reasons — quoted text, template language, citations, or copied material. The teacher reviews the report and context. Your job is to be able to show what work is yours and where your sources came from.

**Where to go:** [Copyleaks — color meanings](https://help.copyleaks.com/s/article/WhatdothedifferentcolorsontheCanvasassignmentmean681cd1ef3d2b5)

<!-- source: RJ Copyleaks Reference; tags: copyleaks, similarity, plagiarism, sources, teacher -->

### Q: Is a low Copyleaks score proof that I'm fine?

No. A low similarity score only means the text didn't match much in the database. It doesn't prove the work followed every academic integrity rule. Your teacher still looks at the assignment, sources, writing process, and class expectations.

**Where to go:** [Copyleaks — Canvas Student overview](https://help.copyleaks.com/s/article/CanvasStudent681cc7aa4d5b2)

### Q: What if I'm failing a class?

Don't hide it. Talk to your teacher, check Canvas for missing work and feedback, and use Academic Support. One or more Fs can affect activity eligibility, and a semester GPA below 2.00 leads to an academic probation review.

**Where to go:** [Canvas Student Guide — Grades](https://community.canvaslms.com/t5/Student-Guide/How-do-I-view-my-grades-in-a-current-course/ta-p/493)

### Q: What is academic probation?

Academic probation can happen if you earn a semester GPA below **2.00** or have poor academic performance. It means the school reviews your situation and sets expectations for improvement. If it applies to you, work with your teachers, counselor, and the Assistant Principal for Academics.

### Q: Can bad grades affect sports or activities?

Yes. To stay eligible, you must pass six classes and may not fail more than one at the end of a marking period. If you're failing one class, you're ineligible for at least one week or until Canvas shows all passing grades, whichever is later.
