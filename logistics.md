# Logistics

Schedules, the Red/White day rotation, lunch, lockers, getting to school, and the start-of-year stuff. Most of this lives on the **My RJ Resource Board** — when in doubt, start there.

### Q: What time does school actually start?

On a regular schedule, first period starts at **8:30 AM**. Before that, **7:45–8:25 AM** is used for Academic Support, clubs, or faculty meetings. Check the My RJ Calendar or Academics tile if the day has a special schedule.

**Where to go:** [My RJ Calendar](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#calendar)

### Q: What time does school end?

On a regular schedule, the school day ends at **2:50 PM**. Early dismissal days end class at **12:50 PM**, with Mission Programming after that. Late starts and IMPACT Days still end at **2:50 PM**.

**Where to go:** [Academics tile](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboarddetail/26269)

### Q: What does Red Day or White Day mean?

Red Day and White Day are RJ's rotating schedule days, and your classes are tied to the day color. A class labeled **1R** meets during first block on a Red Day, and **1W** meets during first block on a White Day. Check the My RJ Calendar to see whether a day is Red or White.

**Where to go:** [My RJ Calendar](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#calendar)

<!-- source: Student Handbook 2025-26; My RJ Calendar; tags: red day, white day, schedule, calendar, classes -->

### Q: How do I know if today is Red Day or White Day?

Check the **My RJ Calendar**. Turn on the Red Day and White Day calendars if they aren't showing, then use month, week, or list view. The calendar also marks closed days, late starts, IMPACT Days, and first-day events.

**Where to go:** [My RJ Calendar](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#calendar)

### Q: When is lunch?

On a regular schedule, lunch is **12:40–1:25 PM**. On an IMPACT Day, lunch is **12:45–1:30 PM**. On a one-hour late start, lunch is **12:55–1:40 PM**.

**Where to go:** [Academics tile](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboarddetail/26269)

<!-- source: Student Handbook 2025-26; Academics tile; tags: lunch, bell schedule, impact day, late start, food -->

### Q: How do lunch accounts and lunch money work?

Your **Student ID card is your lunch card** — it works in both cafeterias and the Steele Center café. You load meal-plan or spending money onto the account, and details live on the **Food Service** tile in My RJ. One thing to know: there's **no credit during the last six weeks** of each semester, so keep the balance topped up.

**Where to go:** [Resource Board](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboard) → Food Service

### Q: What happens on a one-hour late start?

First block (1R/W) runs **9:30–10:35 AM** instead of starting at 8:30. Then there's **Academic Support 10:35–11:00**, 2R/W until 11:40, 3R/W and lunch (**12:55–1:40 PM**), and the day still ends at **2:50 PM**. Check the My RJ Calendar and Academics tile when the schedule looks different.

**Where to go:** [Academics tile](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboarddetail/26269)

### Q: What is an IMPACT Day?

An IMPACT Day uses a special schedule with an IMPACT period built in. First block runs **8:30–9:45 AM**, IMPACT runs **9:55–11:05 AM**, lunch is **12:45–1:30 PM**, and school ends at **2:50 PM**. Check the My RJ Calendar to see when IMPACT Days happen.

**Where to go:** [My RJ Calendar](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#calendar)

<!-- source: Student Handbook 2025-26; Academics tile; My RJ Calendar; tags: impact day, schedule, calendar, lunch, my rj -->

### Q: What happens if it's both an IMPACT Day and a one-hour late start?

RJ has a separate combined schedule for this. First block (1R/W) runs **9:30–10:25 AM**, IMPACT runs **10:35–11:45 AM**, lunch is **1:00–1:50 PM**, and school still ends at **2:50 PM**. The Academics tile shows the full block-by-block version.

**Where to go:** [Academics tile](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboarddetail/26269)

### Q: What is Academic Support? Is it like study hall?

Academic Support is built into the schedule so you can get help, catch up after an absence, or meet with a teacher. On regular days it appears **7:45–8:25 AM** and again in the **9:55–10:20 AM** window. It's a good time to ask questions before you fall behind.

**Where to go:** [Academics tile](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboarddetail/26269)

### Q: Do freshmen get an ID card, and what if I lose mine?

Yes — every student gets a picture ID, and it doubles as your **lunch card**. If you lose it, replacements are made at the **library** for a fee. Keep it somewhere safe, since you'll use it to pay for food.

**Where to go:** [Resource Board](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboard) → Library

### Q: Where do I report a lost item, and where's Lost & Found?

There's a Lost & Found container in the **Dean's Office**. Items are held for a maximum of **7 days** and then donated or discarded, so check soon after you lose something. For a lost **school laptop**, skip this and contact **IT Helpdesk** right away instead.

**Where to go:** [Resource Board](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboard) → Student & School Policies

### Q: Can I switch a class after school starts?

Sometimes, but it's not automatic. Course changes must be approved by the **Assistant Principal for Academics**, and changes based only on teacher preference aren't considered. For schedule or course questions, contact **Mr. Ryan Williamson**, Assistant Principal for Academics, at **303-269-8118** or **rwilliamson@regisjesuit.com**.

**Where to go:** [Academics tile](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboarddetail/26269)

### Q: Where do I get a parking permit?

Parking info lives in **Transportation & Parking** in My RJ. As a freshman, you **can't park on campus**, but you **may buy a permit for an off-campus lot when one is available**. That tile is where the current details and any openings are posted.

**Where to go:** [Resource Board](https://regisjesuit.myschoolapp.com/app/student?svcid=edu#resourceboard) → Transportation & Parking

### Q: Is RaiderQuest the only summer work I have?

No. RaiderQuest is required, but incoming freshmen also need to check **Summer Assignments** in My RJ. All students have summer reading under English, and incoming freshmen complete the freshman English assignment for their division; students in higher-level language courses may have language work too.
